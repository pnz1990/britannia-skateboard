Our History

The Courts skatepark was founded in 2017 and has become a core part of the Vancouver skateboard culture and community.

After learning in August 2021 a skatepark was not included in the re-development of Britannia ("Britannia Renewal") and the Courts was going to be demolished, we got to work by:

  • Meeting with Britannia Executives
  • Presenting at Britannia Committees & Boards
  • Creating and submitting video and written testimonials
  • Raising awareness at community events with info tables
  • Building renderings of what a skatepark "could" look like

Following this, we decided to create an official committee under Britannia Community Services Centre by writing a Terms of Reference & Community Guidelines document. As of the March 9, 2022 Britannia Board of Directors meeting, we are now an official committee that is funded and supported by the community centre.

We thank the Britannia Community Services Centre and look forward to working together!
